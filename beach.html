Beaches in Davao del Sur Province

Davao del Sur offers several coastal spots, primarily located around Digos City and smaller municipalities, known for their authentic, off-the-beaten-path experiences. While Samal and Talikud Islands (often visited via Davao City) are popular, Davao del Sur's coast provides quieter alternatives, including beaches and coastal resorts in areas such as Digos, often characterized by darker sand and local charm.
