SMLanang Premier

SM Lanang Premier is a four-story premier shopping mall located on J.P. Laurel Avenue in Davao City, Philippines. Opened on September 28, 2012, on the site of the former Lanang Golf and Country Club, it features a wide range of retail stores, dining options, cinemas, and event spaces managed by SM Prime Holdings.

Key Features & Amenities

  • Entertainment: Home to the only IMAX Theater in Mindanao, as well as digital cinemas, a bowling center, and an arcade.
  • Retail & Dining: Features major international brands like Uniqlo, Forever 21, and Marks & Spencer, alongside the SM Store and SM Supermarket. The Skygarden offers an outdoor dining experience.
  • Convention Center: The SMX Convention Center Davao is located within the complex, hosting national conventions, trade fairs, and large events.
  • Proximity: The mall is adjacent to Park Inn by Radisson Davao, making it convenient for travelers.

Visitor Information

  • Location: J.P. Laurel Ave., Brgy. San Antonio, Agdao District, Davao City.
  • Operating Hours: Monday – Thursday: 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM. Friday – Saturday: 10:00 AM – 11:00 PM. Sunday: 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM.
  • Parking: Includes an underground parking area.
